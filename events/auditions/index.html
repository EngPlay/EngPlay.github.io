Auditions

Details

The audition will consist of a quick reading of an audition piece. The audition pieces are small parts of the script that the directors have selected to best represent the character. You will do a read through of two audition pieces. One for a main character and one for any of the numerous smaller (but equally important!) characters. The process should take about 15 minutes. Come to the audition with an idea of which character you would like to read for! Ideally, have an idea for a main, a backup main, and any of the smaller roles.
