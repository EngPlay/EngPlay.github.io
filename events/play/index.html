A Death in the Faculty

Synopsis

Fifteen students are gathered at the remote home of an eccentric sponsor to find out the winner of a large grant. Their stay is interrupted by a winter storm, cutting them off from civilzation. Just as the worst of the weather hits, a grisly death occurs. Who could've done it? Find out in this term's EngPlay!
