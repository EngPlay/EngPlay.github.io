Synopsis

The world as we know it has been reborn. A super intelligent AI has achieved sentience and solved every problem in STEM. Three engineering students happened to have missed all of it. Now, they must come to grips with the fact that they are no longer needed as they embark on a hilarious and outrageous quest to understand the enigmatic AI. Along the way, they might just discover something about themselves.
