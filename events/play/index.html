Planet of the Arts

Synopsis

Three engineering students - Austin, Bob, and Claire - lock themselves in an apartment, shut off from the world, to study for exams. When they emerge to restock on food, they come to find that the technological singularity has occurred and an AI now rules the planet. This artificial super-intelligence has solved STEM, making most engineers obsolete, and now the world operates on an Emotions/Arts based economy. When the three discover that it is actually possible to visit the AI's headquarters and meet him, at a very steep cost, they embark on a journey to gather enough "wealth" so that they may meet and face the planet's ruler. Along the way, they face many challenges, both physical and mental as they come to terms with the world around them and what it means for their futures.
