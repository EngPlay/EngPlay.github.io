About

I am a first year Mechatronics Engineering student from the University of Waterloo. Wanting to prepare for the professional world and one day start my own company, UW was the perfect place for me. I am an amateur improv comic, ultimate frisbee player, and knower of random facts.

From building gesture controlled robots to hacking an ordinary light switch into a clapper light, I have developed quite a passion for tinkering with electronics and working on projects that are on the boundary between software and hardware. I am now starting to explore web and app development and I look forward to where engineering will take me.

Myo Controlled Robot

For my first term capstone project, our class was tasked with creating a robot to do SOMETHING. We had a lot of freedom (within certain guidelines) and so my group and I set out to create a gesture controlled fetching robot. The robot was able to move forward, backwards, turn right and left, line follow with a light sensor, and grasp objects to bring them back to us. All of this control was handled with a Myo armband.

  • Purpose: School Project
  • Date: November 2015 - December 2015
  • Type: Robotics and Wireless Communications
